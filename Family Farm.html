Our Concept

We are a family farm,working in collaboration with Fair Back Foundation a Dutch organisation based in Groningen Netherlands. Our aim is to fulfill some of Fair Back's objectives of developing disadvantaged communities in Uganda through sustainable agricultural practices focusing on youth and women.

Vision

We envision an enlightened, self sufficient and prosperous communities.

Mission

To promote sustainable, ecological and socio-economic development among farmers of Abia a sub-region in Soroti, Uganda.

Values

  • Honesty, Integrity and Commitment
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Gender sensitivity

We engage in mixed agriculture

"Sustain the world"

Our slogan is to sustain the world by being mindfull of what we do with agricultural practices. Promote ecological farming so us to maintain our enviroment, therefore our drop in the ocean contribution to a better climate. Create awareness, build an informed and self-sustained community and future generation.
